About Our Mission

Democratizing AI-powered trading insights for everyone

The Problem

Traders are overwhelmed with financial news from hundreds of sources daily. Making sense of market sentiment in real-time is nearly impossible for humans alone. Critical trading opportunities are missed, and decisions are often based on incomplete information.

Our Solution

AI Trading Signal Engine uses advanced Small Language Models (SLM) to analyze financial news sentiment in real-time. We transform unstructured news data into clear, actionable trading signals with confidence scores and risk assessments.

Technology Stack

Built with cutting-edge technologies for maximum performance and reliability:

🤖 Gemma SLM ☕ Java Spring Boot 🐍 Python Flask 🔥 HuggingFace ⚡ REST APIs 📊 NewsAPI

Our Vision

We believe the future of trading is AI-assisted. Our mission is to make sophisticated sentiment analysis accessible to every trader, from beginners to professionals. By combining cutting-edge AI with intuitive design, we're building the next generation of trading intelligence tools.
